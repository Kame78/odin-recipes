Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 150g All Purpose Flour
  • 20g Vital Wheat Gluten
  • 120g Water
  • 4g Salt
  • 3g Sugar
  • 1g Active Dry Yeast

Making the Dough

  1. Add all the dry ingredients to a stand mixer bowl
  2. Pour out your water into a seperate container
  3. Add 75% of the water and knead for 5 minutes
  4. Slowly add the rest of your water and knead for another 10 minutes
  5. Let proof on the counter for 1-2 hours
  6. Cover the dough and allow it to ferment in the refrigerator for 1-3 days
