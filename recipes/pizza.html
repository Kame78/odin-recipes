High Protein Neapolitan Style Pizza

This is a pizza which uses Vital Wheat Gluten in the dough to increase protein and make it healthier


Dough

  • 150g All Purpose Flour
  • 20g Vital Wheat Gluten
  • 120g Water
  • 4g Salt
  • 3g Sugar
  • 1g Active Dry Yeast

Pizza Sauce

  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomatoe paste
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano

Pizza

  • 80 grams fresh mozzerella cheese
  • Pizza dough
  • 115 grams sauce
  • 6-8 pieces of fresh basil

Making the Dough

  1. Add all the dry ingredients to a stand mixer bowl
  2. Pour out your water into a seperate container
  3. Add 75% of the water and knead for 5 minutes
  4. Slowly add the rest of your water and knead for another 10 minutes
  5. Let proof on the counter for 1-2 hours
  6. Cover the dough and allow it to ferment in the refrigerator for 1-3 days

Making the Sauce

  1. Combine ingredients in a sauce pan
  2. Simmer for 10-15 minutes
  3. Allow to cool and put ina sealable container

Assembling the Pizza

  1. Make sure you have a pizza stone
  2. Preheat oven to highest temo it will go
  3. Cover the dough in flour and put on a clean surface
  4. Shape the dough to 12-13 inches wide
  5. Add the sauce to the middle and spread with a spoon
  6. Add the cheese and basil
  7. Put the pizza on a pizza peel and insert into oven
  8. Cook until the crust is crispy and the cheese is nice and melted 6-10 minutes
